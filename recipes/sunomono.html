Sunomono

This is a tangy Japanese style cucumber salad

  • 1 large Japanese cucumber (2-3 english or persian work, too!)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 Tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  1. Slice cucumbers very thin
  2. Toss salt with cucumber and let stand 3 to 6 minutes to draw out moisture
  3. squeeze or pat dry, and rinse if taste is too salty
  4. Whisk vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, and sesame seeds in a bowl until sugar dissolves
  5. Toss cucumbers with dressing and serve
