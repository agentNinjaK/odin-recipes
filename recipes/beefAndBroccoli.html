Beef and Broccoli

  • 1 lb boneless beef chuck, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 cup beef consomme or broth
  • ½ cup of soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • Fresh broccoli florets
  • cooked rice for serving
  1. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the brown sugar, sesame oil, minced garlic, soy sauce, and beef consomme
  2. Add the sliced meat and consomme mixture to the crockpot
  3. Cook either on low for 6 hour or high for 4 hours
  4. 30 minutes before cooking time is over:
    1. take some sauce out of the crockpot and let it cool a bit
    2. mix cornstarch with sauce, add mixture back to the pot
    3. add in the broccoli florets
  5. Serve over rice
